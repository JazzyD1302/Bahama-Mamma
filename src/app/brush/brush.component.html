How To Preparing For Your Airbrush Tan

EXFOLIATE. This is extremely important! Exfoliate your skin the night before or morning of your appointment to remove dead skin cells. Best results occur when exfoliation is done several hours prior to our spray session.

BE CLEAN. Avoid wearing makeup, perfumes, lotions, oils, deodorants or any product on the skin that would create a barrier. Your skin MUST be completely clean & dry for the best results.

SHAVE EARLY. Shave/wax at least 8 hours before your session to minimize irritation.

DRESS APPROPRIATELY. Have dark, loose fitting clothing and under-garments to wear after your appointment.

CHECK YOUR MEDS. Medicated or prescription lotions may act as barriers to tanning solutions. We recommend you wait at least five days from the last use before you get an airbrush tan.

NO SUNBURNS. Sunburns have a possibility of peeling, which will peel your Airbrush Tan off. Do not get an Airbrush Tan until your skin has peeled.

During Your Airbrush Tan

UNDERGARMENTS. You are required to wear bottoms of your choice, tops are optional.

AIRBRUSH PROCESS. The tanning process takes approximately 10-15 minutes to apply in which you'll be airbrushed by hand by our Paasche Airbrush System.

WAIT. You will be asked to remain in the room for 5 minutes before you get dressed to allow the solution to dry completely and evenly.

How To Maintain Your Airbrush Tan

AVOID WATER. Wait 8-12 hours after your tan before showering, sweating or coming into contact with warer.

WEAR LOOSE CLOTHING. After your tan, wear dark, loose fitting clothing for 6-8 hours. (Tight clothing will rub your tan uneven. After your post-tan shower, avoid clothing that chafe or do anything to encourage the skin to shed.)

MOISTURIZE. MOISTURIZE. MOISTURIZE. This is the most important step in maximizing your sunless tan. You can extend the life of your tan by nearly double by using a quality moisturizer morning and evening after first post-tan. We have tan extender lotions available to purchase.

WASH CAREFULLY. Use a moisturizing body wash product, to help keep skin moist. Pat your skin dry after bathing or showering, don't rub.

AVOID EXFOLIATING. Avoid any hard scrubbing or exfoliation in the shower, which will remove or lighten the tan (until you are ready for your next tan).

AVOID PRODUCTS WITH ALCOHOL AND AHA. The most common cause of pre-mature fading is due to the use of products (lotions and soaps) containing alcohol.

BE CAUTIOUS WITH CHLORINE. A sunless tan will almost always fade when you are submerged in any type of water for any length of time. Avoid long hot showers arid baths, and excessive exposure to chlorine in swimming pools and hot tubs.

EASY ON THE SWEAT. Take care when exercising as excess sweat may cause your tan to fade unevenly.
